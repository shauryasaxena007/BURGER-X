About BURGER X

Founded by Shaurya Saxena,BURGER X is Kanpur's premier destination for gourmet burgers. Located in the Heart of Kanpur (208001), we pride ourselves on serving the finest quality burgers made with fresh, locally-sourced ingredients.

Our passion for creating the perfect burger drives everything we do. From classic favorites to innovative creations, each burger is crafted with care and attention to detail.focuses on the delicious food (fresh ingredients, unique flavors, juicy patties, toppings like caramelized onions/sauces), the experience (cozy atmosphere, great service, storytelling), and marketing (social media buzz with captions like "burger o'clock," promotions, local events, behind-the-scenes glimpses) to build a strong brand that attracts customers seeking both quality and fun
